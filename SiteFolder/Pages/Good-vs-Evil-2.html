Good Vs Evil Game 2

How to play

The game plays like rock, paper, and scissors, but with resources.

The player needs to manage 3 resources :

  • health (green)
  • attack (red)
  • defence (blue)

Each player starts up with 100 points for each resource, and the most important resource is health because it determines who wins or loses the game.

The first player to have 0 health points loses!

Gameplay:

The player has 3 options on each round: attack, defence and power up.

  • attack beats power up
  • defence beats attack
  • power up beats defence

Each option will spend resource points!

Except the power-up option which restores 20 points of a resource of player choice.

Advance gameplay:

1. When attack or defence resources are below 0 then the player loses health points according to the negative value of the resource used.

2. The attack and defence options have 3 levels each to spice things up.

  • lv1: consumes 10 points and inflicts 10 points of damage to the other player
  • lv2: consumes 20 points and inflicts 20 points of damage to the other player
  • lv3: consumes 30 points and inflicts 30 points of damage to the other player

Also is used to untie plays, for instance, if the good player chooses an lv3 attack, this will beat an lv2 defense from the evil player, however, the evil player will only lose 10 points of health (the difference).

If both players do the same level of attack or defense then it is a tie, none of the players lose health, however, both players lose resources.
